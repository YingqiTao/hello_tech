Hello Tech!

A guide for young women and non-binary individuals to explore their possibilities and increase their confidence in the tech industry

"What do you want to do when you grow up?" What a fix-minded way of thinking about the future. While we encourgae you to think about what you want to in life, we would like you to know that you are not just that one job, and you do not need to wait until you're "grown up"! Especially in the technology industry! We invite you to learn different types of careers in the tech industry and get involved to see if you really like any of them! Go break into tech and break a leg!
